Humble beginning

Before founding BEROZGAR UNION, the founders experimented with and established two notable but short-lived companies:

GEDISTHAN and SABZI KIRANA.

  • GEDISTHAN was a unique service that enabled users to securely and reliably share and split their app and service subscriptions with others. Our platform made managing shared subscriptions effortless and ensured a seamless experience for all users.

  • During the lockdown, Sabzi Kiranna procured dead inventory from grocery retailers and offered it to customers online at 40-50% discounts. Our service aimed to reduce waste and provide essential goods at affordable prices, ensuring that everyone had access to groceries during challenging times.
